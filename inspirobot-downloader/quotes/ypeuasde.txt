A poor warrior once approached a tired, old witch and asked her "how can it be that you are so beloved, you who are such a tired, old witch?"
"The reason for is that a tired, old witch is only a tired, old witch if the a tired, old witch thinks herself to be a tired, old witch" replied the witch.
The warrior had never before heard such truth this well articulated, and took it to his heart and carried it with him for the rest of his life, and a couple of years later, he was betrayed by his own brother and sentenced to death.
