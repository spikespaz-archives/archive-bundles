Introduction

Welcome! Create your Madlibs story by typing into the text editor below.
You can insert parts of speech and other replacement areas surrounding the request in square brackets.

Example:
The big [color] fox jumped over the lazy [animal].

These square bracket surrounded text can be referred to as "tags". Some common tags are the following:

  • [noun]
  • [adjective]
  • [verb]
  • [color]
  • [animal]
  • [smell]
  • [sound]
  • [taste]
